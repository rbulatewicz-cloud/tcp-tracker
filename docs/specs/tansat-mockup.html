TANSAT — End-to-End Walkthrough

This walkthrough shows the complete TANSAT (Temporary Authorization for No Standing / Tow-Away) workflow from SFTC engineer flagging the need, through MOT building the request packet, emailing Reggie at LADOT, paying the invoice, and tracking spend.

Phase 1 (ships now): email is composed in the app and handed off to the user's mail client (Outlook). Phase 2 (post-IT): automated send via the existing email pipeline.

AI integration: uses existing Gemini 2.5 Flash setup (same as driveway letter scanning). MOT uploads the LADOT invoice PDF and Gemini auto-fills Log #, amount, due date, and customer name.

Roles: SFTC = SFTC engineer · MOT = MOT team (Garrett lead) · Admin
