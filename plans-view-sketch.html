Where do the removed rows go?
Stage pipeline + KPI cards
Already live on the Dashboard. These are "situational awareness" numbers — perfect for a dashboard landing page. They don't need to repeat on a list view where you've already decided to do work.
Cycle time widgets (23.2d, 12.3d, 22.1d)
These are analytical. They belong on the Dashboard and Reports — not on a list view. A distilled version (just the 4 numbers: 165 · 26 · 16 · 9) stays inline in the toolbar.
Summary of changes
Remove the stage pipeline row and the KPI / cycle-time row from Plans view — they live on Dashboard.
Merge the quick-filter pills row and the view toggle into one row.
Collapse the 7 filter dropdowns into a single Filters ▾ button that opens a compact 2-column popover.
Keep the four key numbers (total · at DOT · at risk · overdue) as inline text in the toolbar — zero extra rows.

Result: 5 chrome rows → 2 chrome rows. The table starts higher, more data is visible without scrolling, and the view feels purposeful instead of overwhelming.
